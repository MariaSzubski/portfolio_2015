Urban Walls: Community Revitalization

A Cincinnati Art Project Documentary

About This Project

Elevator Pitch

Gordon Salchow's design was initially intended to be painted on a series of three walls around the Skywalk located at 6th and Race Streets. The wall was painted on the back of the Mabley & Carew building, one of many wall surfaces that were exposed along the West 4th Street district during the early 1970's. Forty years later, public art projects continue to be a component of urban revitalization in neighborhoods throughout Cincinnati.
