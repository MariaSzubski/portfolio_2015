This is a concept for the redesign of the NBC Universal Careers website. The layout features large imagery and easy to use navigation that highlights the benefits of working for the company. The NBC Universal Careers website is for people to Find jobs at NBC and keep track of their job application. The goal was to create a site that is easy to navigate and that highlights imagery from NBC Universal's properties. In the final design, the background is prominent while all of the navigation is grouped along the left hand side. This navigation is broken up into 4 sections: search, content, news, theme park jobs (a separate site). The 'content' itemss expand to show addtional links. The second screenshot show a mockup of a 'Working at NBC' page. The background image would be swapped out for an employee's photo. The text on the right show a breif description of their responsibilities along with a link to a 'Day In the Life' video. Users can navigate to other positions using the headshots at the bottom of the screen. The goal of the layouts was to be modern, fun, and inviting to highlght the benefits of working for NBC Universal.
