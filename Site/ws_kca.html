KCA Wishlist Generator

A Advertisement for KMART and the 2011 Kids' Choice Awards

About This Project

Elevator Pitch

Kmart's Wishlist Generator concept for Nickelodeon's Kids' Choice Awards 2011. Kids enter their stats and favorite shows to find cool items they might be interested in. The design asthetic aligns with KCA 2011 branding, Japanese commercial advertising.

Skills Demonstrated

Web Design

Final Design

The design asthetic aligns with KCA 2011 branding: Japanese commercial advertising. It features bright colors and lots of decorative 'sticker' elements.

This design concept use slot machine metaphor to make it a little more fun and interactive. Kids can pull the slot machine lever to bring up new options.

The design is meant to be fun, welcoming, engaging, interactive.

Once they answer the questions they are presented with a list of items. These items can be dragged and dropped into the wishlist at the bottom of the screen. Once the list is complete is can be shared with friends and family.

Kmart's Wishlist Generator was designed to advertise KMART the 2011 Nickelodeon's Kids' Choice Awards. Kids answer basic questions about their interests and preferences.
