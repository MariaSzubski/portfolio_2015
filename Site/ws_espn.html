About This Project

Elevator Pitch

In celebration the 40th anniversary of Title IX, espnW is creating the largest mosaic of female athletes in history. This microsite allows users to upload Facebook photos and search for friends or sports within the mosaic.

Skills Demonstrated

Web Design

Design

I incorporated the ESPN W branding to create and engaging design. The mosaic design had to be slides while being powerful enough to be the main element on the page. The yellow elements call out the primary functions on the page.

BACKGROUND & PURPOSE In celebration the 40th anniversary of Title IX, espnW is creating the largest mosaic of female athletes in history. This microsite allows users to upload Facebook photos and search for friends or sports within the mosaic. In honor of forty years of Title IX, ESPN W is building the largest female athlete photo mosaic in history. Its user generated. This microsite allows female athetes can upload their facebook photos to add the to mosaic. Users can also search for their friends or a specific sport within the mosaic. CONCEPT I incorporated the ESPN W branding to create and engaging design. The mosaic design had to be slides while being powerful enough to be the main element on the page.
